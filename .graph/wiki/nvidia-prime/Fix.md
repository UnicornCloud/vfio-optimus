For those who don't know how to:
Start Live-System/ISO-Image with internet connected
Choose "Try Ubuntu"
Open "Terminal"
Type:
 sudo apt-get update
 sudo apt-get install apt
Then start installation of Ubuntu with your favourite options - even with third party drivers.
